About

Code4Code is an AI-driven educational platform tailored for computer programming education, offering a comprehensive solution for learning Python and JavaScript. This platform includes the "Code4Code Authoring Tool", enabling instructors to create customized programming exercises.

Code4Code utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to enhance the learning experience, guiding learners through their educational journey based on their personal training experiences. Specifically, the platform employs several machine learning classifiers to support learners, with an additional classifier dedicated to assisting instructors in developing exercises. Central to our discussion is the "AI-based Learning Guide Prediction Model," a key in personalized learning that leverages artificial intelligence to allow instructors and learners to tailor educational experiences to their individual needs and preferences.

Code4Code represents a significant advancement in educational technology, addressing programming education challenges and setting a new precedent for personalized learning. By integrating AI and machine learning, Code4Code enhances engagement and outcomes for learners and exemplifies the transformative potential of technology in education, aligning with the evolving requirements of the digital era.
