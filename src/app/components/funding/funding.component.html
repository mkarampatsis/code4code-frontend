About

CODE4CODE is funded by the Research and Innovation Foundation of the Republic of Cyprus under the "CREATION AND INITIAL DEVELOPMENT OF STARTUPs WITH INTERNATIONAL ORIENTATION (Pre-Seed)" Grant, Call 1221, Program "PRE_SEED" of RESTART 2016-2020. The project was launched on November 1st, 2022 and is expected to be completed on May 30th, 2024.

PROJECT TITLE: A novel Adaptive Learning Platform for Python and Javascript, utilizing an AI-driven learning and content authoring domain structure

PROJECT ACRONYM: CODE4CODE

Project summary:
Code4Code focuses on coding skills training and learning, and aims to bring teachers and learners in control of their knowledge and content-authoring environments. Teaching and learning how to write proper software code has been a challenge for tutors and learners at various educational levels (secondary and tertiary primarily) due to the limitations of available authoring platforms for coding to address the tailored needs of tutors and learners. In the Code4Code project, we will design and develop the first holistic coding learning approach for Python and Javascript that utilizes artificial intelligence (AI) techniques. Information semantics, reasoning, and machine learning (ML) algorithms that learn from real-time interaction with the learning environment will facilitate automatic decision-making feedback mechanisms in remote/self-learning setups. Code4Code introduces a novel Web platform that provides a Coding authoring subsystem for tutors and a self-learning environment for students, supported by an AI/ML module. The Code4Code Platform aims to reach beyond TRL5 by the end of the Project with validations and demonstrations organized in secondary and tertiary educational institutes in Cyprus.
